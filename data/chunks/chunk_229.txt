Keywords: Cultivation, Dristi, Narmada, Spring, Urdbean R ABI/Spring urdbean has an area of 1.01 million ha and production of 0.91 million tonnes with an average productivity of 903 kg/ha in 2021-2022. Major states for spring cultivation of urdbean are Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Assam, West Bengal and Odisha. Spring ecology is important because of higher productivity and better quality of produce in urdbean. IPU19-10 (Narmada) has been thoroughly tested in different agro- climatic regions of Madhya Pradesh, alongwith check varieties, IPU13-1, IPU10-26 and KU 96-3. The overall performance of the variety at all locations, showed yield superiority of 16.61%. The variety was tested in Betul, Narsinghpur, and Hoshangabad districts of Madhya Indian Farming 74 (02): 07-08; February 2024 Pradesh. It has wider adaptability, suitable for cultivation across different agro-climatic regions of Madhya Pradesh. Narmada was thoroughly tested in AICRP across all the zones in the country in IVT and AVT 1 during spring 2020 and spring 2021 and its yield varied from 802 kg/ha to 2130 kg/ha.
