 The online electronic payment in a completely secured IT environment, helps common users/citizen from the hassle of going to banks for making drafts and then to Government offices to deposit the instrument for availing the services. It also helps avoidable delays in the remittance of these instruments into Government account as well as eliminates undesirable practices in the delayed deposit of these instruments into bank accounts. NTRP facilitates instant payment in a transparent environment using online payment technologies such as Internet Banking, Credit/Debit Cards. NTR Portal has been functional in new Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare since inception in FY 2019-20.
