Papaya   Papaya plants should be grown in 40-60 mesh net houses. Crops like brinjal, pumpkin should not be grown around the papaya field.   Before planting plant should be sprayed with acephate 1.5 g/lit in nursery. Installation of blue and yellow sticky traps (10-15 per acre) should be done to avoid the spread of white fly.   Application of Boardax mixture 1% or aliette 2g/l for controlling of papaya foot rot   To avoid the spread of sucking pests, spraying of neem oil 2.5 ml/lit and acephate 1.5 grams + gum 0.5 ml per lit recommended at 15 days interval.   To increase the quality in virus affected plants spraying of urea 10 grams, zinc sulphate 1.5 grams, boron 1 gram per lit is recommended at 30 days interval.   Eradication of virus affected plants is to be carried out. Pomegranate   Spraying liquid paraffin at 1% concentration at 15 days interval twice during June reduces   fruit cracking. Guava   High yielding variety Arka Kiran can be followed.   Planting is done from June to December at a spacing of 5-6 m either way.
