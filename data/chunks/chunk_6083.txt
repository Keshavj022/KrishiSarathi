It is applied externally for itch; a decoction of plant is used as a wash for stones. The seeds are alterative and are used in purgative preparation. A cold infusion is given as a depurative. Nagphani: A parasitic climber, common throughout India and Sri Lanka up to an altitude of 8,000 ft. sometimes completely covering bushes and trees.
