Seed treatment: Soak the seeds in Carbendazim or Pyroquilon or Tricyclozole solution @ 2 g/kg seeds/l water for 10 hours and drain excess water. Bio-fertilizers: Soak the seeds in a solution containing 1 kg each of Azospirillum and Phosphobacteria or 1kg of Azophos bioinoculants overnight.
