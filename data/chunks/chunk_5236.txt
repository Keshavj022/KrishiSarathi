It is sold in the market, used as medicine and is collected for personal consumption as food (Table9). This Lavi fair is the biggest trade fair in inner Himalayas held at Rampur Bushahr in Shimla district of Himachal Pradesh. Raw and semi furnished wool, woollens, patti (light blankets), pashmina, chilgoza and guchhi are sold in this fair.
