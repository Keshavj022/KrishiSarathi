Maruca management: Application of 5% NSKE or neem oil @ 5 ml/l should be taken up before flower bud initiation or at 35 DAS to avoid egg laying by Maruca adults. Spray either thiodicarb 75 WP @ 1.0 g/l or profenophos 50 EC @ 2.0 ml/ lt which has ovicidal action at the time of flower initiation. First spray with ovicides should be given one week before flower initiation as and when the adult population is noticed in the crop. Spray acephate 1.0 g or chloropyriphos 2.5 ml or quinalphos 2.0 ml or thiodicarb 1.0 g per litre at the time of flowering stage.
