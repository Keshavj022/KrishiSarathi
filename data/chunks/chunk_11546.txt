 ICAR RABI AgRo-AdvIsoRy foR fARmeRs 403 Spray fungicides and pesticide in combination for effective management of thrips and purple blotch incidence. Spray Schedule includes Mancozeb @ 0.25% with Methomyl @ 0.8 g/l at 30 days after transplanting, Tricyclazole @ 0.1% with Carbosulphan @ 2 ml/l at 45 days after transplanting, Hexaconazole @ 0.1% with profenofos @ 1 ml/l at 60 days after transplanting. While taking onion seed production, farmers are advised to follow isolation distance of 500 to 1000 meters from different onion varietal plots to maintain seed purity. Bulbs should be treated with Carbendazim 50 WP @ 1gm./lit before planting. It is recommended to install 2 honeybee colonies for higher seed yield Chilli: After the harvest of red chilli, dry the harvested produce on concrete platforms or tarpaulin or polythene sheets for 8 to 10 days to avoid aflotaxin and get dirt free good quality produce.
