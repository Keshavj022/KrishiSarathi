 Examples include tomatoes and ginger in Nepal (Kopp and Mishra, 2022), vegetables in Madagascar (Minten et al., 2009), and tomatoes in Senegal (Maertens et al., 2010). Based on the results of this paper, policymakers in those contexts could consider implementing institutions similar to India’s mandi system.
