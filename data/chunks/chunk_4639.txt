SUMMARY Pusa Gynoecious Cucumber Hybrid-18 is a gynoecy based hybrid of cucumber with earliness and desirable horticultural traits for spring summer and kharif season cultivation in Zone IV (Sub-humid Sutlej-Ganga Alluvial Plains i.e. Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Bihar and Jharkhand).
