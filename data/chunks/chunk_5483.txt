Unclassified Search Inventory of Indigenous Technical Knowledge in Agriculture Document 2 (Supplement 1) Chapter 1 Rain water management Code No. Title of the ITK Description of the ITK Name and address of the discloser/facilitator 2322 Conservation of rain water in small ponds The rain water is conserved by digging a small pond of 1/8th (0.05 ha) acre, size. The water is used for irrigating the garden plants after the rainy season, i.e. from January to March. The farmers of Haripur village of Puri district in Orissa are using small ponds for irrigation since 10 years.
