 ICAR agro-advisory for KHARIF 2022 166 Small Onion   High yielding varieties CO (On) 5 and CO 6 are recommended for kharif season.   Plant in May-June after treating the bulb with Bacillus subtilis @ 5 g/kg + Trichoderma asperellum @ 5 g/kg   Apply T. asperellum@ 2.5 kg/ha with VAM @ 12.5 kg/ha to reduce the basal rot incidence   Foliar spray 0.5% ZnSO4, 1% MnSO4 thrice on 30, 40 & 50 days after sowing.   Five days after planting, raise barrier crop maize in 2 rows around the field and ridges at a spacing of 1 feet, to prevent the entry of Thrips   To manage thrips incidence, install yellow sticky traps @12/ha, spray Dimethoate 30 EC @ 1.3 ml/lit or Methyl demeton 25 EC @ 2 ml/lit.   To control leaf spot or purple blotch disease, spray mancozeb @ 2 g/l or copper oxychloride @ 2.5 g/l.   Foliar spray IIHR vegetable special twice. Brinjal   Varieties: CO 1, CO2, COBH .1 and CO BH.2 for Coimbatore, Erode, Tirupur and Salem, MDU 1 for Madurai, PKM 1 for Theni and Madurai, PLR 1, PLR (Br) 2 for Cuddalore, KKM 1 for Tirunelveli, PPI 1 for Kanyakumari and TNAU Brinjal VRM 1 for Tiruvallur, Vellore and Thiruvannamalai districts of Tamil Nadu.   Treat the seeds with Trichoderma viride @ 4 g / kg or Bacillus subtilis @ 10 g / kg seed and with Azospirillum @ 40 g / 400 g seeds using rice gruel as adhesive   Apply Bacillus subtilis to soil @ 2.5 kg/ha with 50 kg FYM to reduce disease incidence   Follow IPM practices for the management of brinjal fruit and shoot borer. Apply 8-10% neem oil mixed neem cake @ 100 kg/ac or neem cake @ 250 kg/ha at last ploughing to reduce fruit and shoot borer incidence. Install pheromone traps @ 12/ha, spray Azadirachtin 1.0% EC (10000 ppm) @ 3 ml/lit or Emamectin benzoate 5 SG @ 4 g/10 lit.   Foliar spray ZnSO4 @ 0.5% thrice at 10 days interval from 40 days after planting. Bhendi   Sow during June to August @8 kg seeds for varieties and 2.5 kg seeds for hybrids (CO 4 and Arka Nikita)   Follow 45 x 30 cm spacing in ridges and furrows and 90 x 45 x 45 cm for paired row system in drip irrigation.   Treat the seeds with Trichoderma viride @ 4 g/kg or Bacillus subtilis @ 10 g/ kg of seeds and 400 g of Azospirillum using starch as adhesive and dry in shade for 20 minutes.   Treat the seed with imidacloprid 70 WS @ 7g/kg seed to prevent leaf hopper and spray Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 100ml/ha or Thiamethoxam 25 WG @ 100 g/ha.   Spray Oxyflourfen at 0.15 kg ai/ha or Fluchloralin @ 1.0 kg ai/ha or Metolachlor @ 0.75 kg a.i/ ha as pre-emergence application on third day of sowing.   Foliar spray 1% urea (10 g/l) + 1% muriate of potash (10 g/l) on 30 and 45 days after planting.
