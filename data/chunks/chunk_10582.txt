Impact on Household Income Comparison of household income before and after the interventions is shown in Table 11. The average income of farm households more than doubled (2.4 times) between 2016-17 and 2020- 21. The horticulture experienced 4.6 times increase and became the most important source of household income (45.56%) replacing field crops whose share fell to 31.94%. Farm and non-farm enterprises, the third important contributor to household income, experienced 3.3 times increase.
