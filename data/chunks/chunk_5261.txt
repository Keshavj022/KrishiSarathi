 The traditional houses are not in fashion 2. Modern technique of house building is very quick and cheap 3. poor sanitation and light in traditional houses 4. traditional houses require more time to build 5. Non availability of wood and slate stones 6. Skilled masons for traditional construction are rarely Available 7. There is always a fear of fire. Perceived reasons for less or no damage to the traditional house due to earthquake. 1. Stone are used through Jod-Tod technique for wall making. 2. Wood structure gives support to the stones. 3. The foundation of the house is better. 4. The hinge joint of wooden frame is strong and has shock absorption capacity. 5. 'Konia' technique is used at the outer side corners of the house. 6. The walls are thick. 7. Thin and flat stones do not displace from the wall. CONCLUSION The present study conform the in-built characteristics of wood and stone houses to resist seismic forces.
