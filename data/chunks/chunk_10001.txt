Impact on Household Income Comparison of household income before and after the interventions (Table 43) indicate that, the average income of farm households more than doubled (2.5 times) between 2016-17 and 2020-21, increasing the net income by 150 per cent during this period. Livestock and horticulture, being the dominant sources of household income, experienced an increase in income by 206.83 and 136.65 per cent, respectively.
