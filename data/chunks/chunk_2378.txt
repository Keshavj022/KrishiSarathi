Basis for preparation of Accounts: The Accounts are prepared under the historical cost convention unless otherwise stated and generally on the accrual method of accounting. 2. Revenue Recognition (A) The following items are accounted for on accrual basis while recognizing revenue; i. Pension and Leave Salary contribution-on raising demand ii.
