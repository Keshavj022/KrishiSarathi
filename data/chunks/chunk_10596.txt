Impact on Household Income The household income before and after the interventions was compared (Table 29). The average income of farm households more than doubled (2.9 times) between 2016-17 and 2020-21. The increase in income from livestock was 3.5 times (243.73%) followed by fisheries, which increased by three times during this period.
