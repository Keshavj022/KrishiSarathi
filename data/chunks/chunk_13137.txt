India Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) is the apex public research organization which has been playing a key role in the innovation processes concerning agriculture. Traditionally, ICAR has played its technology development and dissemination role, limiting itself to the frontline demonstrations in the KVKs as an outreach activity. But with growing agriculture-dependent economy, it is also important to commercialize potential technologies enabling first generation entrepreneurs to take up agri-startups in order to accelerate the goal of doubling farmers' income by 2022.
