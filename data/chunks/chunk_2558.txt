1 Proceedings of the Annual Conference of Vice Chancellors of the Agricultural Universities and Interface of ICAR Directors January 17-19, 2014 Annual Conference of Vice-Chancellors (VCs) of the Agricultural Universities (AUs) was held at Baramati, on February 17-19th2014. The Directors of ICAR Institutes, and Senior ICAR Officials were also present on 19th Feb., 2014 during the interactive sessions. 17th January, 2014. An informal meeting was held at City Inn hall under the chairmanship of Secretary (DARE) & DG, ICAR. The new Vice Chancellors were introduced during the meeting. The Vice- Chancellors were invited to make their observations on the quality of agricultural education in the country. The following points emerged during the discussion: o Shortage of faculty either due to non-availability of qualified persons or lack of support from the authorities. It was desired that the vacant positions be filled up immediately with suitable candidates, as the State Governments have already been sensitized about this issue. o The efforts should be initiated to include agriculture education in RUSA and the issue may be taken up with MHRD. It was also mentioned that respective State Governments need to be approached in this regard. o ICAR may renew support for farm modernization. It was mentioned by ADG (EQR) that the proposal has been included in the XII Plan EFC memorandum of the Education Division. The Chairman informed about approval of NAEP by the Department of Economic Affairs, Govt. of India. He stressed that Agriculture Universities must adopt the Model University Act as it is directly linked with the support to be provided under NAEP.
