Initiatives one-payment The e-payments system in all Pay & Accounts Offices of Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare had been successfully implemented from 2011 onwards. e-Payment System Since, the IT Act, 2000 recognizes the digitally signed documents or electronic records digitally authenticated by means of an electronic method or procedure in accordance with the provisions of section 3 of the Act, the Controller General of Accounts had developed a facility in COMPACT for electronic payment (e-payment) through digitally signed electronic advices. This had replaced the existing system of payment through cheque while leveraging the COMPACT application running in all Pay & Accounts Offices in all Ministries/Departments of Central Government. The e-payment system developed was a fully secured web based system of electronic payment services which introduces transparency in government payment system.
