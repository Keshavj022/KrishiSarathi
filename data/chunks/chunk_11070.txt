It has provided not only developmental assistance and technical guidance but has helped in developing appropriate institutional models and research strategies. In the furtherance of its objectives, the ICAR receives budgetary support from the Government under the bye-laws of the ICAR, by way of: (i) Government of India grants; (ii) Proceeds from the Agricultural Produce Cess Act; and (iii) Other sources (Internal Resource Generation) Due to merger of Plan & Non-Plan Budget from the year 2017-18, the Government’s classification will be done on Revenue and Capital.
